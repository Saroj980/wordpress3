About NBTC

The “National Brain Tumor Center” has been established at Bir Hospital in Kathmandu, Nepal, with the primary objective of delivering exceptional care, conducting brain tumor research, and raising awareness. A multidisciplinary team of specialists collaborates to provide comprehensive care, utilizing state-of-the-art technology and advanced diagnostic tools for accurate diagnoses. The center envisions being a beacon of hope, offering individualized treatment plans and support for patients and their families throughout their treatment journey. The ultimate goal is to provide a standard of care that maximizes positive outcomes for all patients.
